Orb-Jump (WIP)

Orb-Jump is a game currently being developed for PC and Mobile. The aim of the game is to get as high up the screen as possible by jumping from orb to orb, avoiding hazards such as blackholes. This game is made with one simple control and that is touch. simply time your touches in order to succesfully project yourself in the direction of another orb, failure to land on an orb results in floating for eternity...

Ice-Cream Tycoom (WIP)

Ice-Cream Tycoon was a game developed over the course of a 9-month period for my multimedia major project. It is a tycoon game where they player acts as an overseer of an Ice-Cream business that starts with just a simple stand, and eventually earns money to progressively upgrade your stand more and more until you become the "Ice-Cream Tycoon". You must manage storage of ice-cream, ice-cream cones and also keeping up with the current trends in order to successfully grow your buisness.
