Procedural Terrain Generation Tool - C++, OpenGL, GLSL

This is a tool that generates a terrain by reading in the information from a height-map, and using the black and white pixels to determine where to raise and lower the terrain mesh. This also features an underwater post-processing effect, custom shaders for the water and terrain, a particle system/emitter and skeletal animation with FBX loading, all coded from the ground up!
