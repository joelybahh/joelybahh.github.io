Latest Project

Welcome To Wonderland - VR!

A VR challenge where you need to use your wits and puzzle solving skills to survive and escape the galaxy's most dangerous game show and stay one step ahead of its charismatic, crazed, omnipresent host. Welcome to Wonderland.

New

You play as a contestant in the Galaxies most popular and dangerous game show, Welcome to Wonderland. The overall goal is to collect all the keys to unlock the escape pod and ultimately, escape the show itself.
The keys are collected by going through every ‘level’ and solving the puzzles/challenges in order to earn that levels key (You acquire this key inside the level). The keys are also used as a way to unlock the Arcade Zone, which is the contestants only source of fun and entertainment! However, this zone isn’t handed to them for free, every machine/game in that zone has to be earned through puzzle completion and challenges.
Once the player has earnt all the keys, they can unlock the space door and escape the show once and for good!

Things I worked on!

The VR Claw Machine logic - Omni-Directional Level using physics, Claw Automation etc.

Elevator Asynchronous load in VR - Allows for each level to load around the player while they are in the elevator!

Physics-Based Gumball Machine Logic - Setup the gumball machine to work with physics, so the gumballs roll around the spiral, and into the dispenser.

Physics Based Flick Switches - Setup logic for flick switches snapping to the left and right.

Physics Based Light Switches - Setup logic for the light switches snapping to on and off.

VR Locomotion Prevention - Can't teleport while heads clipping through a wall, Can't teleport when your controller is obstructed from view.

VR Body Awareness - Detects changes in height on the terrain and pops you up, allowing players to walk up and down stairs in VR.

Ticket Swipe and Tap Mechanic - This feature gave the player the option to swipe or tap their card in VR to progress.

Breakable Items Logic - This feature works similarly to Unity's LOD system, however instead, calculates what impact velocity is required to go to the next damage state.

Gravity Toggle System - A very simple mechanism, it takes in an array of rigidbodies, and sets their gravity to true or false depending on the puzzle state.

Teleporting With Items - Programmed the logic for teleporting with items you're holding.

Teleporting With a Bucket Of Items - Programmed the logic for teleporting with items you're holding that are in a container/bucket.

Custom Physics On The Wheel of Fortune Needles - Programmed the logic for helping unity physics accuratly collide with the needle that slows down the wheel.

Puzzle Tracker Logic - Programmed the logic for accurately tracking the players puzzle interactions. This communicates with the Voice Line system to play correct voice lines depending on their ability to solve puzzles.

Teleporting With a Bucket Of Items - Programmed the logic for teleporting with items you're holding that are in a container/bucket.

THE ABILITY TO WEAR HATS! - I was the mad man behind the hat wearing mechanic! A simple but fun implementation to the game.

THE ABILITY TO SMOKE CIGARS! - I was also the mad man behind the cigar smoking! I do not encourage anyone to take up smoking.
